# 📚 Remedia Hook Library — 100 Viral-Mechanic Hooks

> **20 ready-to-use hooks per market × 5 markets = 100 hooks**
> Companion to `Remedia_Production_Guide.html` Step 10. Use one hook per video; do not reuse the same hook ID until 10 weeks have passed.

---

## How to Use This Library

1. Pick the next unused hook ID for the market you're producing.
2. Paste the **script** into Lipsync Studio (Step 4 of the production guide).
3. Match the **suggested B-roll #1** to your hook's body-part or visual subject — this becomes the 0.0–1.5s scroll-stopper.
4. Use the **Hook Variant** universal motion prompt (not the Calm Variant).
5. Save the rendered file as `Kit[N]_[Category]_H[N].[ID]_v1.mp4` per Step 10's naming convention.
6. Track results in your weekly review sheet.

## Categories (Scroll-Stop Mechanic)

| Tag | Mechanic |
|---|---|
| `BodyPart` | Fixates on a body part — viewer touches it instinctively |
| `Symptom` | Opens with a specific symptom — "if you wake up with X" |
| `Counter` | Counter-narrative — "this isn't aging, it's X" |
| `DidYouKnow` | Historical/scientific reveal opener |
| `Cliffhanger` | Withholds info until end — "watch what happens" |
| `Authority` | References the tradition's track record |

## Universal Constraints (All Hooks Honor These)

- ✅ Specificity over vagueness (named herbs, named body parts, named protocols)
- ✅ Cultural authority by attribution (Ayurveda, TCM, Kampo, Andean) — never ethnic essentialism
- ✅ Modern science cited as confirming the tradition (not contradicting it)
- ✅ Comment-to-DM CTA in every hook
- ❌ No "[ethnic group] never…" formula
- ❌ No CANCER WARNING / LAWSUIT graphics
- ❌ No specific brand attacks (defamation risk)
- ❌ No "in just one night" miracle claims for medical conditions
- ❌ No vaginal lemon, kidney-stone "flushing," or other dangerous protocols
