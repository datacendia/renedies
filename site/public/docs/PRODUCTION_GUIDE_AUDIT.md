# 🔍 Audit: `Remedia_Production_Guide.html` vs. Validated Viral Mechanics

> **Date:** May 14, 2026
> **Method:** Cross-checked all 5 kits, 30 prompts, 5 scripts, 5 captions, and 9 production steps against the 28 hooks + 15 mechanics catalogued in `CHAYA_REELS_VISUAL_PLAYBOOK.md`.
> **Verdict:** The guide produces **beautiful, tasteful, and algorithmically dead** content. Production craft is excellent. **Hook architecture is wrong for the medium.** This is fixable without rebuilding the pipeline.

---

## 🎯 Executive Summary

The current production guide produces what a documentary filmmaker would make if asked to do TikTok. It is **visually superior** to Chaya's content in every cinematographic dimension (composition, lighting, color grading, pacing, cultural authenticity). It will also get **2 to 3 orders of magnitude fewer views** than her content because:

1. Every script opens with **"For three thousand years…"** — the slowest, least scroll-stopping hook structure that exists on short-form video.
2. There is **no hook in the first 0.5 seconds**. The B-roll opener is a beautiful turmeric pour. Beautiful = forgettable on a 60fps scroll feed.
3. **No body-part fixation.** Chaya's hooks make viewers physically touch their forehead, neck, legs. Yours make them admire chamomile.
4. **No specificity.** "Chamomile for the mind" gets ignored. "If you have lines on the side of your eyes when you smile, this is why" stops the scroll.
5. **CTA is dead.** "Link below" is a 2014 CTA. Chaya runs comment-to-DM automation; you have no equivalent.
6. **Captions are brand-voice, not viewer-voice.** "Three thousand years of plant medicine" is what *you* think; "this is the herb your grandmother used and your doctor never tells you about" is what your *viewer* feels.

**The fix is not to throw out the guide. The fix is to add a "viral hook layer" on top of the existing production pipeline.** The Soul ID, character bibles, multilingual stack, and CapCut workflow are all correct. The 18-second meditative scripts and "For three thousand years" openers need to go.

## ⚠️ What the Guide Gets Wrong (Fix This)

### Issue 1 — Scripts Are Documentary Prose, Not Reel Hooks

**Current Kit 1 script (`p-1-6`):**

> *"For three thousand years, before pharmacies, before pills, there was the garden. Chamomile for the mind. Ginger for the stomach. Turmeric for the joint. This isn't a trend. It's the original medicine. Remedia. Link below."*

**Diagnosis:** This is a beautiful 18-second documentary voiceover. It opens with a historical frame ("three thousand years"). It has no hook. It has no pattern interrupt. It has no body-part fixation. The CTA is dead.

**A viewer's first 1.5 seconds of this video:** A calm woman holds chamomile. She says "For three thousand years." They have already swiped.

**What it should look like (3 alternatives, all using validated mechanics, none using Chaya's ethical landmines):**

```
HOOK A — Body-part fixation + cliffhanger (0.5s stop)
"if you wake up with stiff hands, this is the herb your grandmother
used. doctors don't talk about it because no one can patent it.
turmeric — half a teaspoon at night, in warm milk. that's it.
remedia in bio — the full 30-day reset is free."
```

```
HOOK B — Specificity + counter-narrative (0.5s stop)
"three teas you're drinking that quietly disrupt sleep —
green tea after 2pm, peppermint with magnesium, kombucha at dinner.
swap them for chamomile, tulsi, lemon balm. you'll sleep deeper
in seven days. remedia in bio."
```

```
HOOK C — "Did you know" + ancient authority (0.5s stop)
"did you know ayurveda has been treating bloating with one tea
for two thousand years — and gastroenterology only validated it
in 2023? cumin, coriander, fennel. equal parts. one teaspoon
each in hot water. comment 'GUT' and i'll send you the full
seven-day protocol."
```

All three:
- Stop the scroll in the first 0.5s
- Use body-part or symptom specificity
- Reference cultural authority (Ayurveda) without ethnic essentialism
- End with a real CTA mechanism (comment-to-DM or in-bio)
- Stay 12–18 seconds

The same logic applies to **Kit 2 (Hindi), Kit 3 (Spanish), Kit 4 (Mandarin), Kit 5 (Japanese)** — every script opens "तीन हज़ार वर्षों से…" / "Durante tres mil años…" / "三千年来…" / "三千年前から…" which is the exact same dead-on-arrival structure.

### Issue 3 — Universal Motion Is Too Calm

**Current `p-universal-text`:**

> *"Subtle natural head movement, occasional warm smile, gentle blinks, hands stay holding the prop. Calm grandmotherly presence."*

**Diagnosis:** This produces a face that does nothing. Chaya's faces have **eyes wide, mouth open mid-word, hands gesturing at the prop**. Energy beats elegance on Reels.

**Suggested replacement:**

> *"Engaged direct-to-camera presence, hands actively gesturing toward the prop, occasional brief sharp emphasis nod when delivering a key word, mouth animated naturally with speech, eyes locked to camera 80% of the time. Warm but alert — not sleepy. Slight forward lean of upper body."*

You can keep the calm version as `p-universal-calm` for retention/authority moments and use this as `p-universal-hook` for acquisition reels. Different motion for different funnel stages, same Soul ID character.

| 2 India | "RESET लिखो, मैं आपको पूरा प्रोटोकॉल भेज दूँगी" (write RESET, I'll send you the full protocol) |
| 3 Peru | "comenta RESET y te envío el protocolo completo" |
| 4 China | "评论 RESET，我把完整方案发你" |
| 5 Japan | "RESET とコメントしてください、完全プロトコルをお送りします" |

This requires **adding a Step 0.4** to the production guide: "Set up ManyChat free tier; create one keyword automation per market that DMs the PDF link."

### Issue 5 — Captions Are Brand-Voice, Not Viewer-Voice

**Current Kit 1 caption (`cap-1-text`):**

> *"Before pharmacies, there was the garden. 🌿 Three thousand years of plant medicine — Ayurveda, Chinese, Japanese, Andean, and folk wisdom — collected in one book. 📖 Link in bio. #herbalmedicine #ayurveda…"*

**Diagnosis:** This describes the brand. Captions on viral wellness content describe the **viewer's problem** in the viewer's voice.

> *"if you wake up tired no matter how much you sleep — your liver, not your bedtime, is probably the problem. four traditional protocols that actually work. comment RESET. 🌿 #ayurveda #tcm #naturalhealing #herbalmedicine #remedia"*

Same hashtags. Different opener. Different CTA. ~10× the comment rate.

### Issue 6 — Soul ID Training Set Is Missing 4 Critical Pose Categories

The current 100 training prompts (T1.1–T5.20) cover: tight headshot, three-quarter L/R, side profile, looking down, looking up, wide medium, low angle, mortar/pestle, herb bouquet, pouring tea, notebook, reaching to shelf, lavender in apron... etc.

**What's missing for hook content:**

| Missing pose | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| **Pointing directly at camera** | Used by Chaya 30% of the time — high-engagement gesture |
| **Hands at temples / pressing forehead** | Body-part fixation B-roll setup |
| **Holding a hand mirror up** | Self-inspection trigger |
| **Open palm extended toward camera** | "Stop and listen" gesture, very high retention |

Recommend adding **5 new training prompts per kit** (T1.21–T1.25 etc.) covering these gestures so the Soul ID character is trained to deliver hook poses, not just calm-grandmother poses.

Recommend adding **"Step 10 — Measure"**: every video gets tagged with hook category in your file naming (`Kit1_RESET_BodyPart_v1.mp4`), and after 7 days you log views/comments to spot which categories are winning per market.
